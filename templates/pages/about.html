About Me

Critical Thinking in the AI Era

Hi, I'm Angel Medina, a Full Stack Rust Engineer who believes that in an era where AI can generate code, what truly matters is critical thinking, problem solving, and the ability to communicate complex ideas clearly.

I'm not your typical developer. My journey combines hospitality work at Cuvee with production level Rust engineering. This unique blend gives me something rare: the technical depth to architect scalable systems and the soft skills to explain them to anyone.

Currently, I'm building FlightMate v0.3, a production ready flight deal platform serving 380 US cities, complete with regional email clustering, passwordless authentication, Stripe payments, and comprehensive admin systems. It's the kind of project that demands both deep technical expertise and careful decision making.
